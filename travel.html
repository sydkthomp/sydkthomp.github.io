Why I Travel

There's nothing quite like stepping off a plane into a brand new country. From the cobblestone streets of Lisbon to the volcanic peaks of New Zealand, travel has shaped who I am. It's taught me that the world is bigger, more beautiful, and more connected than I ever imagined.

Having lived in Cancún, Mexico as a missionary, and adventured through Europe and New Zealand, I've learned that the best souvenirs aren't things — they're memories, friendships, and a little more understanding of the world.

Places I've Been

Europe

A whirlwind trip through some of the most iconic cities in the world. Every country had its own flavor, charm, and unforgettable moments.

  1. France 🇫🇷
    • Saw the Eiffel Tower light up at night
    • Explored charming Parisian cafes
  2. Amsterdam 🇳🇱
    • Biked along the famous canals
    • Visited beautiful historic museums
  3. London 🇬🇧
    • Saw Big Ben and Buckingham Palace
    • Rode the iconic red double-decker buses
  4. Portugal 🇵🇹
    • Wandered the colorful streets of Lisbon
    • Tasted the most amazing pastéis de nata
  5. Spain 🇪🇸
    • Soaked in the vibrant culture and cuisine
    • Practiced Spanish with locals

New Zealand 🇳🇿

New Zealand was pure magic — dramatic landscapes, adventure around every corner, and the friendliest people I've ever met. Oh, and the ice cream? Life-changing.

  1. Hiking Adventures
    • Hiked breathtaking trails through lush greenery
    • Climbed actual volcanoes — still can't believe I did that
  2. Swan Lake
    • Visited a gorgeous lake filled with black swans
    • One of the most peaceful moments of the whole trip
  3. The Food
    • Ate ice cream basically every single day
    • No regrets whatsoever
